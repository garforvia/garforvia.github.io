Constitutional posture

Republican now, but shaped by historical transitions.

Garforvia’s founding identity was monarchical in 2020. Over time it passed through mergers, reform periods, and constitutional shifts. The present system is republican and environmentalist, but the site should not erase that political evolution.

That is useful because it gives the state a more credible institutional backstory than a blank-sheet micronation that appeared yesterday with a flag and nothing else.

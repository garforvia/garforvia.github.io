History

A state shaped by merger, separation, collapse, and restoration.

Garforvia’s history is one of transformation rather than static continuity. That is not a weakness. It is the backbone of the national narrative.

Restoration symbolism
The arms help bridge the founding, merger, separation, and restoration narrative.
National timeline

History with continuity

The site treats Garforvia as a state with phases, not a one-note creation myth.

Flag continuity
The state colours help tie together different constitutional eras.
August 2020

Foundation of Garforvia

Garforvia emerged as a new micronational entity, originally framed around monarchical principles and experimental political organisation.

2021

Growth and consolidation

The state expanded its governance structure and population, building institutional momentum.

Later period

Merger with Nesforth

In pursuit of alliance and stability, Garforvia merged with the Leeds-based micronation of Nesforth, creating Nesforvia.

After separation

Transformation into Altorosia

The political landscape shifted again, producing Altorosia, which later played a role in the creation of Serarin in Crofton.

20 March 2026

Official restoration of Garforvia

Garforvia was formally restored, reclaiming its identity and resuming its place in the micronational sphere.

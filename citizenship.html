Civil Registry

Apply for Garforvian citizenship

Submit an official citizenship application to the Civil Registry. Each application receives a reference number, enters the review queue, and can be tracked online.

Process

How the workflow is framed

1. Submission
Applicant details are received and a reference is generated.
2. Review
The Civil Registry examines eligibility, seriousness, and identity confidence.
3. Decision
Status changes to approved, pending further information, or declined.
4. Registration
A citizen identifier is assigned and recorded in the national register.

Registry guidance

Before you apply

Review period
Applications are examined by the Civil Registry and may be approved, held pending, or declined.
Citizen number
Approved applicants receive a Garforvian citizen identifier which can then be used for passport requests.
Status tracking
Keep the reference issued on submission. It is the fastest way to check progress.

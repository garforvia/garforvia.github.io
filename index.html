National overview

What Garforvia is

The Republic of Garforvia is a micronation located in Altofts, West Yorkshire. It describes itself as an environmentalist republic with a robust governmental structure, a legal system, and a digital citizenship framework.

Founded in August 2020, it passed through institutional changes, mergers, separations, and reforms before its restoration on 20 March 2026. That history matters because it gives the state a genuine narrative of continuity, fracture, and return.

Name and origin. “Garforvia” is presented as descending from the phrase “Garforth Village”, connecting the modern state identity to local place-memory, fertile land, and historical settlement.
